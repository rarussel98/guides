Scan Pipeline

Content intelligence system. Videos in, knowledge out. Multi-platform, one brain.

What is this?
You watch videos everywhere — TikTok, YouTube, podcasts. Some contain ideas worth keeping. Scan captures those videos, transcribes them, categorizes them by topic, and synthesizes everything into playbooks you can search and reference.

Think of it as a second brain for video content. Instead of bookmarking 500 videos you'll never rewatch, scan extracts the knowledge and organizes it for you.

3 ways to capture a URL
⌨️

Terminal (Mac Mini)

Auto-detects platform, queues for next run

scan add https://youtube.com/watch?v=abc
💬

Claude chat (any device)

Creates a Notion inbox entry. Picked up automatically on next scan or scan pull

\scan add https://youtube.com/watch?v=abc
📱

TikTok (phone)

Just favorite the video. Run scan tiktok later and favorites get scraped.

Commands

All run on 🖥️ Mac Mini. Alias scan in ~/.zshrc

Ingest
scanStatus + run all queued platforms
scan tiktokTikTok Safari favorites flow
scan youtubeYouTube URL file flow (also: scan yt)
scan add <url>Queue any URL (auto-detects platform)
scan pullPull URLs captured from Claude chat
Process
scan reviewInteractive triage: keep, delete, recategorize
scan publishPush approved videos → Notion + playbooks
Maintain
scan rebuildRegenerate playbooks (selective by default)
scan rebuild --allFull rebuild of all 7 playbooks
scan backfill 50Enrich older videos (batch of 50)
scan statusPipeline stats across all platforms
scan sortMigrate legacy batch_1 videos

Daily workflow
1

Collect

Throughout the day, favorite TikToks on your phone. Copy YouTube URLs when you find something good. Or tell Claude to scan add a URL from any chat.

2

Process

At the Mac Mini, type scan to download, transcribe, and categorize everything that's queued.

3

Triage

Run scan review to approve the good stuff and delete the noise. Category by category.

4

Publish

Run scan publish to push approved content into Notion pages and playbooks.

5

Grow

Over time, your 7 playbooks become comprehensive reference guides. Searchable, organized, always growing.
